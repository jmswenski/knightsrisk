Castlewatch Platform

Incident coordination, built for trust

Castlewatch is the private incident-sharing workspace for church security teams that need structured reporting, trusted partner coordination, clear review workflows, and detailed operating guidance in one platform ecosystem.

Turn observations into accountable security workflows.

Use Castlewatch to submit incidents, preserve reviewer notes, coordinate follow-up sightings, and keep organization-level decisions tied to explicit state changes.

Designed for trust-based teams, the platform keeps onboarding, organization settings, moderation, billing, and partner relationships in one controlled workspace.

The Castlewatch knowledge base is part of the offering: administrators, reviewers, and reporters get practical reference material for each workflow instead of relying on informal handoff.
